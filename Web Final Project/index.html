CRISTERO ROOTS

  1. The Cristeros were a group of people who fought for Christianity
  2. The Cristero war started in 1926 because the current president of Mexico at the time made Catholicism and Christianity illegal and made it impossible for you to showcase your faith.
  3. These Christians defended their faith by:
    • Risking their lives to literally fight against the government.
    • Doing daily prayers to Jesus and intercesion of Virgin of Guadalupe
    • They would pubicly showcase their faith even though they might've gotten killed
  4. What it inspires me to do:
    • Their story makes me proud to be a Christian from Mexico and pushes me to always have faith in God no matter what.
    • Their story inspires me to always defend my faith even if people hate me for it.
